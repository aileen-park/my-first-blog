My Life

I am a 16 year old girl who currently lives in New York.

I attend a public high school and am planning on majoring in computer science or mechanical engineering when I get to college.

When I was 7 years old, I was diagnosed with non-Hodgkins Lymphoma.

I was forced to miss most of class during 2nd grade, and I made daily visits to the hospital to receive chemotherapy, and I lost all of my hair.

While at home, I began to write songs to express my emotions, because I believed that it was healthier than bottling all my thoughts.

So with that, I became a singer song-writer, developing my techniques by focusing on classical singing and piano.

I hope to find an occupation that will incorporate both my love for music and my creativity in computer science.
